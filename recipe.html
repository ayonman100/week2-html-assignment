How to Make Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish known for its rich flavor and vibrant color. This guide will walk you through a simple way to prepare it at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Red pepper
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Blend the tomatoes, pepper, and onions.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry the blended mixture.
  3. Add seasoning and allow it to cook.
  4. Add washed rice and stir properly.
  5. Add water and cook until the rice is soft.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
